RHOVONIDIN

by

AHSHABAZZ

FADE IN:

A strong BARITONE voice breaks the silence!

VOICE
(Accusing) Charged with acts of inhumanity, Lance Rhovonidin, you are hereby sentenced to perish!!

FADE UP:

EXT. THE BEACH - THE ISLAND - DAY

A worn and ragged man, bleeding, LANCE RHOVONIDIN, stands bound IN broken chains, moored on the beach, facing the lush treeline. which leads to a jungle. A broken boat lies nearby in the waters.

A crashed reconnaissance starship, behind, looms just beneath the waves, while ANOTHER takes off toward the stars!

FURTHER DOWN THE SHORE

Thousands of wild and screaming raiders interact further down the shores!

A female raider spots Lance! 

FEMALE RAIDER
Hey! You, there! (to her colleagues) We got another one!!

They approach and surround him!!

BOSS RAIDER
Let's finish him off, then turn in for the eveening!!

// Lance DISPATCHES the raiders //
// A mysterious islander, small man, in his late seventies, discovers him //

MYSTERIOUS ISLANDER
Against those raiders, that was a masterful job! What is your name?

LANCE
I am intergalactic general, Lance Rhovonidin. I was sent here to perish.

Mysterious Islander helps him address his wounds.

MYSTERIOUS ISLANDER
What did you do? For them to send you here? I can send you help!

LANCE
I don't need anyone's help!

MYSTERIOUS ISLANDER
Then perhaps you can be of help? You can read a map? You can chart the area! Remove any threats. I know a way off this planet. The translocator I need, and the beacon are somewhere over those hills. If you succeed, others will follow you. What do you say to this deal? I'll get the first few items you'll need...
